# Data Sovereignty

## Overview
Data Sovereignty mandates keeping data and AI processing within national borders to ensure regulatory compliance, privacy, and security. In 2026, 75% of European/Middle Eastern firms prioritize it amid EU AI Act and energy reckoning, driving regional AI clouds and Confidential AI.

## Details/Key Facts
- Geopatriation: Data residency for sovereignty.
- Ties to EU AI Act: Auditability/governance.
- Opportunities: Sovereign AI platforms (hybrid/edge).
