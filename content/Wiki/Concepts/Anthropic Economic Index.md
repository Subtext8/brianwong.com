# Anthropic Economic Index (AUI)

Geo/demographic AI usage index revealing uneven adoption.

## Overview
Measures per capita AI usage vs expectations; high in high-income regions (Singapore 4.6x, Canada 2.9x); lower in emerging (Nigeria/India focus on coding).

## Details/Key Facts
- Washington D.C. 3.82 (doc editing/career); Utah 3.78 (tech); California 2.13 (IT/marketing).
- US: 40% employees use AI at work (up from 20% 2023); shifts from augmentation to automation.
