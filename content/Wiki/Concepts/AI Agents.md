# AI Agents

AI that reasons, plans, acts autonomously to goals (perceive-reason-act-observe loop).

**From** [[Wiki/Sources/10-AI-Concepts|10-AI-Concepts]] 

- Chatbot: Answers. Agent: Does (e.g. books flight).
- Ex: [[Entities/OpenClaw|OpenClaw]] (local, connects apps; 60k GitHub stars).
- Use cases: Lead gen, expense tracking (receipts→DB), automations.
- Tools: n8n (no-code), OpenAI agents SDK (code), Madness.

## From [[Sources/Ways-to-Use-AI-in-Daily-Life|Ways-to-Use-AI-in-Daily-Life]]
- Many: prototyping, customer service, reports, invoice mgmt, etc.
