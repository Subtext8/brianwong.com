# Skills Earthquake

## Overview
The Skills Earthquake describes workforce shifts from execution to human-AI hybrid roles. AI handles routine/automation; humans focus on review, ethics, decision-making. Gartner: 50% orgs use AI-free assessments by EOY 2026. Wage premium +56% for AI fluency.

## Details/Key Facts
- Atrophy risk: Critical thinking from over-delegation.
- Shift: Augmentation → Automation → Orchestration.
- Demand: AI skills 7x growth; oversight > execution.
