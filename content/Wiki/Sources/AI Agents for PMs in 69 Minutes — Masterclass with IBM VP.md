## Summary
In this masterclass, Armand Ruiz, VP of AI Platform at IBM, breaks down the current state of AI agents, enterprise AI implementation, and the evolving role of product managers. He emphasizes the importance of technical literacy, the power of open-source AI, and the necessity of systematic evaluation (evals) in agentic systems.

## Key Claims
- AI agents are the next step after the chatbot era, delivering true automation.
- The four building blocks of an AI agent are Thinking, Planning, Action, and Reflection.
- RAG is essential for injecting updated enterprise data into LLMs.
- Evals must be a continuous process integrated into every step of the agentic pipeline.
- Technical literacy is mandatory for leaders and practitioners to understand the "art of the possible."
- Open-source AI is superior for enterprise use cases due to flexibility, control, and cost-efficiency.
