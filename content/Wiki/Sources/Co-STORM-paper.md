## Source: Co-STORM-paper

- **Topic**: Collaborative STORM (Co-STORM) for complex information seeking.
- **Entities**: Yucheng Jiang, Yijia Shao, Dekun Ma, Sina J. Semnani, Monica S. Lam, Co-STORM, WildSeek, STORM, RAG Chatbot, Google Search, You.com, DSPy, Prometheus 2.
- **Concepts**: Unknown unknowns, collaborative discourse, multi-agent systems, mind map, information-seeking assistance, mixed-initiative approach, perspective-guided question asking, moderator agent.
- **Key Claims**:
    - Co-STORM emulates educational collaborative discourse to help users discover "unknown unknowns" with less mental effort.
    - It uses a multi-agent system (experts + moderator) and a dynamic mind map to track discourse.
    - WildSeek dataset (100 data points, 24 domains) was created for evaluating complex information-seeking.
    - Human evaluation shows 70% preference over search engines and 78% over RAG chatbots.
    - Co-STORM outperforms baselines in depth, novelty, and engagement.
