## From [[Wiki/Sources/Stanford-STORM]]
- STORM (Synthesis of Topic Outlines through Retrieval and Multi-perspective Question Asking) automates research by simulating conversations between a Wikipedia writer and a topic expert.
- It uses perspective-guided question asking to improve depth and breadth.
- Implemented using [[DSPy]].
