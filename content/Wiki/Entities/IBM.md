# IBM

## Summary
IBM is a major player in the enterprise AI space, focusing on providing flexible, secure, and governable AI platforms.

## Key Contributions
- **Granite Models:** A family of efficient, cost-effective AI models designed for enterprise use.
- **AI Governance:** Tools for managing AI use cases, compliance, and regulatory requirements.
- **Flexibility:** Emphasis on deploying AI anywhere, from on-prem to cloud environments.
