**Alex Hormozi Profile**

Alex Hormozi is a first-generation Iranian-American entrepreneur, investor, philanthropist, author, digital marketer, podcaster, and public speaker.[^1][^3][^5] He started his first brick-and-mortar business in 2013.[^1]

**Education & Early Involvement**  
Graduated Magna Cum Laude from Vanderbilt in 3 years; served as President of Pi Kappa Alpha Fraternity and Vice President of Vanderbilt Powerlifting.[^2]

**Business Ventures**  
Founder of [[Wiki/Entities/Acquisition.com|Acquisition.com]]; Co-Founder of Skool.com. Focuses on scaling businesses (5M Instagram followers).[^4]
