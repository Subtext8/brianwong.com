## WildSeek

WildSeek is a dataset constructed to study users' interests in complex information-seeking tasks in the wild.

## From [[Sources/Co-STORM-paper]]
- Contains 100 data points across 24 different domains.
- Collected from the open-source STORM web application.
- Used to evaluate information-seeking assistance tools like Co-STORM.
