# My Public LLM Wiki
[[TOC|Kaparthy LLM Wiki]]- *Live Demonstration*

This is a live demonstration of how I keep and maintain a LLM wiki using the Kaparthy LLM Wiki method. This wiki, together with the AI prompts and schema (which is equivalent to the claude.md file), is here for you to review and adapt to your own workflow. I use Obsidian Copilot for this workflow because some wikis I keep are very personal and I want to keep the LLM local. You could use any LLM model. Most demonstrations I've seen out there use Claude Code. The choice is yours; the method is very flexible.

## What is LLM Wiki?
This entire wiki is managed by an LLM based on a particular set of instructions under the schema folder.All the articles or videos I find relevant to my AI study are saved in the Raw folder. Once the LLM "ingests" the source, it is saved as a processed document under the Source folder. An overview is created and updated in the Overview folder. The LLM then extracts every entity and concept into individual atomic nodes. When I query the finding and find new connections, it creates new concepts, entities and comparisons. All activities of the LLM are recorded in the log. The table of contents organizes all the notes within the wiki. Over time, the Wiki grows and updates itself and created new connections and insights depending on what is ingested.
